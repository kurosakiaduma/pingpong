Rules of Ping Pong Game

Follow these instructions to learn how the Ping Pong Game works:

  • Numbers divisible by 3 become "PING"
  • Numbers divisible by 5 become "PONG"
  • Numbers divisible by 15 become "PING-PONG"

NOTE: All non-numberic input do not work with the Ping Pong Game
